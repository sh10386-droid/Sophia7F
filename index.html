Fudgy Matilda Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients: Chocolate Cake: 2 cups all purpose flour (260g), sifted 1.5 tsp baking powder 1.5 tsp baking soda 1 cup granulated sugar (200g) ⅔ cup light brown sugar (135g) 1 tsp salt 3 large eggs, ideally room temp ⅔ cup vegetable oil (133g) ⅓ cup buttermilk - mix ⅓ cup whole milk (80g) + 1 teaspoon vinegar 1 cup sour cream (240g), ideally room temp ¾ cup cocoa powder, Dutch Processed preferred (75g) 2 tsp instant coffee ¾ cup boiling water (180g) Chocolate Fudge Frosting: 300g dark chocolate 3 tbsp corn syrup 8 oz cream cheese, room temperature (226g) ⅓ cup cocoa powder (33g) 1 cup powdered sugar (100g) ¼ tsp salt 1 ¼ cup heavy cream, chilled
